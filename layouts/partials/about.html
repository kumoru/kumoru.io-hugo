Manage & Scale Your Infrastructure

Kumoru does the things that you'd rather not by taking away everything but writing code from your list of responsibilities. Whatever cloud you want to run on, Kumoru handles every step of the process, including:

  • VM creation and management
  • Networking and load balancing
  • Orchestration and scheduling
  • Redundancy and fallback
  • Metric-based autoscaling
  • Application performance monitoring

Monitor & Contain Your Cloud Costs

Kumoru monitors the costs of all of the infrastructure you use to keep your applications running, and rolls all of those costs into one easy-to-read monthly bill across all your cloud providers. Kumoru can break those costs down to show you which groups of applications are using the most resources, giving you the insight to better plan where to spend your time optimizing.

When you set a budget, Kumoru will never allow your monthly bill to surpass that amount. It notifies you when your estimated monthly costs are approaching your limit, giving you time to react — by increasing your budget, optimizing your applications, or preparing for less performance during traffic spikes.

Easy to Integrate

Robust and easy-to-use APIs allow you to integrate Kumoru in to your CI/CD workflow any way you see fit. Hooks let you programmitcally deploy your application from other systems without having to rewire your entire process.
